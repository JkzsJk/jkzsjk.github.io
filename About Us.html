Behind the Scenes

Northern Fame Bookstore (NFB) is best known for being the favourite Malaysian local retail and distributor for all kinds of books, established since 1993. We serve walk-in customers in our shop located at Bukit Jalil, Malaysia. We provide books based on a wide variety of genres our customers preferred. Customers may purchase books in bulks and bundles or just one at a time. Feel free to drop by our outlet to shop for books to your heart's content. We have also put up this website to serve our customers better, especially for those who are not able to drop by our shop physically. Fret not as this website has been built to let everyone drop by our store and shop virtually. It would be wonderful for you to do us a favour. Have a look around at our virtual bookshelf on the home page and happy reading!
